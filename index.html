Facts About Pizza

Origin: Pizza, as we know it today, has its origins in Naples, Italy. It was initially a simple dish made with flatbread topped with oil, herbs, and cheese.

Varieties: There are countless variations of pizza around the world, ranging from the classic Margherita (tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, basil) to unique combinations like Hawaiian (ham and pineapple) or BBQ chicken.

Popularity: Pizza is incredibly popular globally. In the United States alone, it's estimated that about 3 billion pizzas are sold each year.

Top Toppings: Some of the most popular pizza toppings include pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, sausage, and green peppers. However, toppings can vary widely based on regional and cultural preferences.
